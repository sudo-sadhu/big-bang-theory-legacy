Show Milestones

A brief look at the journey of television's biggest comedy.

2007

The Big Bang Begins

The series premieres on CBS on September 24, introducing the world to Sheldon, Leonard, Penny, Howard, and Raj.

2010

The Family Grows

Melissa Rauch (Bernadette) and Mayim Bialik (Amy) become series regulars, expanding the core group to seven and changing the show's dynamic forever.

2014

Ratings Juggernaut

The show reaches its peak viewership in Season 8, averaging over 20 million viewers per episode in the US alone.

2017

Young Sheldon Born

The successful spin-off prequel 'Young Sheldon' premieres, exploring Sheldon Cooper's childhood in East Texas.

2019

The Final Bow

The series concludes on May 16 with a highly emotional, critically acclaimed hour-long finale watched by 18 million viewers.
